The New York Times and Politico report that Andrii Artemenko met with Trump campaign officials after he traveled to Cleveland for last summer’s Republican ational Convention. The Times adds that Artemenko says that “he saw in Mr. Trump an opportunity to advocate a plan for peace in Ukraine — and help advance his own political career.” (Artemenko would later deliver to the White House a peace proposal via Trump attorney Cohen, outlining a way to remove U.S. sanctions on Russia one week before Flynn’s resignation.)
