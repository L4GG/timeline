Mike Pence tells John Dickerson in an interview on Face The Nation that he has spoken with Michael Flynn about Flynn’s communications with Sergey Kislyak. According to Pence, Flynn did not discuss U.S. sanctions against Russia with Kislyak.

“[W]hat I can confirm, having spoken to [Flynn] about it, is that those conversations that happened to occur around the time that the United States took action to expel diplomats had nothing whatsoever to do with those sanctions,” Pence said.

Source: CBS News
