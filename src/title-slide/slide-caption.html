Lawyers for Good Government (L4GG) was created on Election Night 2016 to harness the energy, passion, and talents of the legal community, to fight for a just society and a transparent government that protects civil and human rights and expands opportunity.

L4GG serves Americans whose rights are at risk, and the attorneys and organizations who help them. L4GG collaborates with advocacy and lawyers groups; leverages pro bono resources to engage in impactful legal action and persuasive communications; convenes coalitions to efficiently coordinate resources; and educates the public and decision makers.
