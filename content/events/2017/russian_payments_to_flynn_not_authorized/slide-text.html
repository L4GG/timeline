Acting Secretary of the Army Robert Speer responds via letter to the House Oversight Committee's request for the Pentagon to investigate whether Flynn, a retired Army general, violated Pentagon rules requiring retired officers to report income from foreign states, and states that the Pentagon has not found any documents authorizing Michael Flynn's 2015 Russian government-paid trip to Moscow.

During that trip, Flynn gave a paid interview with the state-run television network, RT, and attended a RT-sponsored gala where he was seated next to Putin.
