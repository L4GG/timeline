Michael Cohen, Special Counsel to the Trump Organization since 2007, hand- delivered a sealed proposal to Michael Flynn's office outlining a way for Trump to lift sanctions against Russia. Cohen has connections to Ukraine through his Ukrainan wife and past family business dealings there. He also participated in deals by the Trump Organization in Russia. The FBI counterintelligence is reviewing his links with Russia.

Michael Cohen, Felix Sater and Andrii Artemenko support the plan. Cohen and Sater said they had not spoken to Mr. Trump about the proposal and have no experience in foreign policy. Artemenko said he had received encouragement for his plans from top Putin aides.

Source: New York Times
