Sean Spicer confirms the phone conversation between Michael Flynn and Sergey Kislyak. “They exchanged logistical information on how to initiate and schedule [a call between Trump and Putin after the president-elect was sworn in],” Spicer told reporter. “That was it, plain and simple.” He said the call followed a text message exchange initiated by Flynn to wish Kislyak a Merry Christmas.
