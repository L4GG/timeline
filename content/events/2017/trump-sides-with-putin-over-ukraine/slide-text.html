One day after Trump's call with Putin, renewed fighting breaks out in Ukraine between Russian-backed separatists and Ukraine's military. Trump appears to side with Putin to cast doubt as to whether the Russian government is supporting Russian-backed separatists fighting in eastern Ukraine.

Trump says of the recent clashes, "we don't really know exactly what that is."

The Russian government has denied involvement in the three-year conflict, but there is evidence it has provided equipment and fighters to the separatists; whereas the Ukrainian army has been trained and equipped in part by the U.S.
