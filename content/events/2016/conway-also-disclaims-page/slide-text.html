Kellyanne Conway told CNN’s Jake Tapper that Carter Page is “certainly not part of the campaign that I'm running, meaning we don't have him — we have a number of people, fabulous people, men and women, as part of our national security and foreign policy team. And he's not among them at Trump Tower." With respect to Carter Page's contact with the Kremlin she said, “If he's doing that, he's certainly not doing it with the permission or knowledge of the campaign, the activities that you described. . . . He is certainly not authorized to do that.”
