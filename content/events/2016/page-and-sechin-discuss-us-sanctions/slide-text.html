During the same trip to Moscow where he addressed the New Economic School, Carter Page also met with Igor Sechin, former Russian deputy prime minister and current chairman of the Russian oil conglomerate Rosneft. Sechin was one of Russian officials and businessmen sanctioned over Russia's “illegitimate and unlawful actions in the Ukraine.” The Treasury announcement described Sechin as “utterly loyal to Vladimir Putin — a key component to his current standing.” At their meeting, Sechin raised the issue of the lifting of sanctions, the intelligence source claimed.
