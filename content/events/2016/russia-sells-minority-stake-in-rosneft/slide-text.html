The Russian government announces it will sell nearly 20 percent of state-run oil company, Rosneft, to Swiss commodity trading firm, Glencore, and the Qatar Investment Authority for approximately $11.3 Bil.  As of January 2017, the owners of Glencore were still unknown.

The deal suggests foreign investors are reconsidering U.S. sanctions against Russia following election of Trump. At the time, Trump was considering Exxon-Mobil Chairman Rex Tillerson for secretary of state. Tillerson had been critical of the sanctions.
