Russia’s deputy foreign minister Sergei Ryabkov told Interfax news agency that the Russian government maintained "contacts" with Trump's “immediate entourage.” He also said, “We continue to do this and have been doing this work during the election campaign,” he said. The Russian foreign ministry later said there were no contacts with Trump's campaign staff, but rather with American politicians and supporters, and Trump spokesperson. Hope Hicks denied the campaign was aware of any contacts between campaign representatives and Russian officials prior to the election.
